Below is the Wajarri Alphabet. Some sounds are represented by two characters. These are known as Digraphs. If you don't know how they are pronounced, they can lead you to say things the wrong way if you read them like they were letters as English uses them.

a b d g e
j l m n ng
nh ny r th u
w y rd lh rl
ly rn rr

Vowels and Consonants

Vowels and consonants are the two main 'types' of sound. Consonants are made by restricting airflow, and vowels are porduced by allowing air to flow freely. Below are some videos showing you how to produce the sounds in Wajarri.
